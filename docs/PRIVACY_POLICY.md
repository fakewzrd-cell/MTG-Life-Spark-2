# Life Spark — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 20 July 2026  
**Developer:** Federick Vidot  
**Contact:** Use the in-app Feedback screen, or the support email listed on the Google Play listing.

Life Spark (“the App”) is an unofficial Commander tabletop companion for tracking life, counters, and local multiplayer games. On Android the package id is `com.lifespark.app`.

## Summary

- We do **not** create user accounts.
- We do **not** sell personal data.
- We do **not** show ads or use third-party analytics SDKs in the App.
- Most game and profile data stays **on your device**.

## Information we process

### Stored on your device
The App stores locally (using on-device storage) information such as:

- Display name / profile preferences
- Decks and match history you create
- App settings (format defaults, haptics, appearance, etc.)
- Local game session state while you play

This data remains on your device unless you choose to clear app storage or uninstall the App.

### Camera
If you use **Join Game**, the App requests camera access to scan a host QR code. Camera frames are used only to decode the QR code on-device. We do not upload photos or video to our servers.

### Network
The App may use network access for:

- Loading Magic card images / metadata from **Scryfall** (when you browse or display card art)
- **Local Wi‑Fi multiplayer** between devices on the same network (host/join via WebSocket). That traffic stays on your local network and is not sent to Life Spark cloud servers (the App does not operate a Life Spark account backend).

### Optional feedback
If you use Feedback, you may open your email client to send a message. That email is handled by your mail provider, not stored inside Life Spark servers.

## Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Third parties that may receive data only as needed for the features above:

- **Scryfall** — card image/data requests when the App fetches card art (subject to Scryfall’s own terms/privacy).
- **Other players on your local network** — when you host or join a game, session/game state is shared with those devices for multiplayer.

Google Play may collect standard install/usage statistics according to Google’s policies when you download from Play.

## Children

Life Spark is a general-audience utility. It is not directed at children under 13. Do not use the App if you are under the age where you can consent to these practices in your region.

## Your choices

- Deny camera permission if you do not need QR join (you may still use other features).
- Clear app data or uninstall to remove local profile/match data from the device.
- Leave a multiplayer session anytime from the App.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.
